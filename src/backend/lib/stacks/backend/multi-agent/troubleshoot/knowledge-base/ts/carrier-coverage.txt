== PLATFORM POLICIES ==

Return policy:
If a label was not used within 30 days of generation, you may request a refund of 90% of the value as platform credit. Used labels are non-refundable.

Lost or damaged package claims:
1. Report within 72 hours of the estimated delivery date
2. Provide tracking number, photos of packaging (if applicable), and description of contents
3. The platform manages the claim with the carrier
4. Resolution time: 5-15 business days depending on the carrier
5. Maximum compensation: according to each carrier's policy

Balance policy:
- Account balance does not expire
- Funds are reflected within 1-24 hours after payment confirmation
- No cash refunds — only platform credit
