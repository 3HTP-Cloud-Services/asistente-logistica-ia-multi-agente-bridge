FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS — SHIPPING PLATFORM

What is this platform?
This is a multi-carrier shipping platform for e-commerce sellers. It allows you to quote, generate shipping labels, and track shipments with multiple carriers from a single place.

Which carriers are available?
We currently work with: Carrier A (national express), Carrier B (national standard), Carrier C (national + international), and Carrier D (same-day metro areas).

How do I get a shipping quote?
Provide the origin zip code, destination zip code, and package weight. The system will show quotes from all available carriers so you can choose the best option.

How do I generate a shipping label?
After quoting, select your preferred carrier and confirm the shipment details (sender, recipient, weight). The label is generated automatically and the cost is deducted from your balance.

How do I track my shipment?
Provide your tracking number and the system will retrieve the real-time status directly from the carrier.

How do I add funds to my account?
You can add funds via bank transfer or deposit. Once the payment is confirmed, the balance is reflected in your account within 1-24 hours.

What should I do if my package is lost or damaged?
Contact platform support with your tracking number. We will manage the claim directly with the carrier. Resolution time depends on each carrier's policy.

Can I schedule a pickup?
Yes, when generating a label you can request a home pickup. Availability depends on the carrier and the zone.

Are there weight or dimension limits?
Yes, each carrier has its own limits. Check the carrier coverage section for specific limits per carrier.
